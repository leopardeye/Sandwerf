Workshops at Sandwerf

Ignite Creativity

Nestled in the heart of the tranquil Namibian Savannah, Sandwerf is not just a venue; it's a canvas for inspiration and creativity. As an artist, coach, health instructor, or chef, you'll find our serene and inspiring atmosphere the perfect backdrop for your creative workshops, allowing you to relax and focus on your craft.

Imagine hosting your workshop in a space surrounded by the calming energy of nature. At Sandwerf, we offer diverse settings, including a creative gallery space, a function/dance hall, outside coaching areas, a garden stage, a chapel, a stunning restaurant, organic gardens, and a production kitchen. Each space is meticulously designed to foster a calm and focused environment, allowing minds to open and creativity to flow freely.

Whether you're planning a weekend workshop or an extensive month-long course, Sandwerf accommodates your needs. Our 13 freestanding chalets, featuring 16 private double rooms, provide comfortable lodging for your participants. Immerse yourself and your students in a weekend of creativity or engage in an extended learning experience amidst the tranquil surroundings.

We understand that each workshop is unique. Sandwerf offers tailored packages for different durations and group sizes. You can book our various spaces exclusively for the required days, ensuring an uninterrupted and focused environment for your participants.

Whether you're conducting a painting class, wellness retreat, or culinary workshop, Sandwerf is the canvas where your creativity can flourish. Connect with us to discuss your vision, and let Sandwerf be the backdrop for an immersive and transformative workshop experience.
