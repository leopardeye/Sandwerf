Equine Photoshoot

Capture the beauty of Joseph's Dream Stud in a 1-hour equine photoshoot with JD. Immerse yourself in the magic of these magnificent creatures, creating memories to last a lifetime.

Stable Tour

Book a 45-minute tour with JD for an inside look at our stables. Explore the world of horses and deepen your understanding of these magnificent creatures

Horsemanship Introduction

Dive into the art of horsemanship with JD. Book a 1-hour session to learn about trust, communication, and the unique bond between humans and horses.

Cooking with the Chefs

Embark on a culinary journey with Chef Elias. Join Hilya for delightful baking sessions, each lasting 2 hours. Discover the joy of creating scrumptious treats with our talented chefs.

Hiking with or without a Guide

Discover the beauty of Sandwerf through invigorating hikes. Whether you prefer the freedom of solo exploration or desire insights into our flora and fauna with a guide, our hiking trails offer a connection to the land. Embrace the serenity of the landscape and let each step be a journey into the heart of Namibia's natural wonders.

Cycling Route

Bring your own bikes and explore our biking route for a refreshing outdoor adventure. Discover the beauty of Sandwerf on two wheels, free for all biking enthusiasts.

Processing Raw Fleece

Unleash your creativity in a 1-2 hour session transforming raw fleece into spun yarn. Discover the ancient craft of wool processing with our skilled artisans.

Exclusive Sundowner

Choose between a snacks and drinks version or a romantic picnic dinner for two at our stunning sundowner spot. Revel in the breathtaking views as the sun sets over the Namibian landscape.
