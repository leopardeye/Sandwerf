Weddings at Sandwerf

Embark on Your Love Story

Your love story deserves a setting as unique as the bond you share. Sandwerf, situated just 90 km from Windhoek, offers an exclusive venue immersed in the natural splendor of the African Savannah. With the capacity to comfortably accommodate eighty guests, we ensure that your celebration at Sandwerf is intimate and unforgettable. As we host only a limited number of weddings each year, your marriage promises to be a truly exclusive experience.

Exchange your vows in our mountain chapel, a serene space perched on elevated terrain, providing breathtaking views of Joseph's Dream Stud Stables. The open design invites the natural surroundings into your ceremony, offering a picturesque backdrop as the sun sets over the Savannah. Whether you're an equestrian enthusiast or captivated by nature's grandeur, our mountain chapel sets the stage for a unique commitment.

After the ceremony, immerse yourself in a photoshoot at Joseph's Dream Stud Stables. Bathed in the golden light of the setting sun, our unique and colourful horses become a stunning backdrop, mirroring the warmth of your love. This enchanting opportunity ensures timeless couple portraits, capturing the essence of your special day.

As night falls, continue your celebrations under the expansive African sky. Sandwerf's Restaurant Courtyard complex, inclusive of the Restaurant and Dance Hall, becomes exclusively yours for the evening. Indulge in excellent cuisine, dance beneath the stars, and create cherished memories that will linger in your hearts forever.

To enhance your experience, our wedding venue is available only in conjunction with accommodations, ensuring a comfortable stay for you and your guests. Wedding photoshoots are reserved exclusively for those celebrating their special day at Sandwerf. Due to high demand, early booking is advised. Explore our exclusive wedding packages and embark on your journey of love in the tranquil embrace of Sandwerf.
