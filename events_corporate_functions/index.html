Corporate Functions at Sandwerf

Elevate Your Professional Odyssey

Escape the ordinary and embrace innovation at Sandwerf, where modern sophistication meets the untamed beauty of Namibia's Savannah. Our Function Hall provides an ideal venue for corporate functions, offering a refreshing environment, professional service, excellent food, and easy accessibility just 90km from Windhoek via a tarred road.

Find clarity and concentrate on your objectives in a revitalising environment that fosters innovation and teamwork. Sandwerf's contemporary interior design, harmonising with the natural surroundings, cultivates a space where ideas can flourish naturally.

Experience the zenith of professional service at Sandwerf. From conceptualisation to realisation, our committed team ensures that every aspect of your corporate function is meticulously crafted. We comprehend the significance of flawless events and collaborate closely with you to fulfil your goals.

Treat your guests to culinary excellence at Sandwerf. Our in-house catering service delivers a diverse and exquisite menu showcasing the flavours of Namibia. Enjoy meals that tell a story of sustainable practices and community spirit, providing a delightful culinary experience for all attendees.

Escape the city hustle and choose Sandwerf for your corporate functions. Located only 90km from Windhoek, our venue is accessible via a tarred road. It is a convenient yet extraordinary getaway for your team.

Whether planning a day function or a stay-over event, our Function Hall is available. Immerse your team in a focused day of workshops or extend the experience with an overnight stay. Please note that for functions at Sandwerf, only in-house catering is permitted.

Elevate your corporate functions with Sandwerf's exceptional venue and service, combining the tranquillity of nature with the professionalism your event deserves.
